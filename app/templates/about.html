Although I temporarily stepped away from coding, I was later drawn to the role of a backend developer—the one who works behind the scenes to stably support a service.

Beyond just writing code, I've come to think deeply about how my services can provide value to users. I'm committed to continuous learning and growth, aspiring to be a developer who creates services that bring convenience and joy to people.
